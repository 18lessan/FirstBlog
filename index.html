ACM TechNews Article

I read the article called, No Strings Attached for Underwater Video System, that highlighted to advancements in video recording underwater. Through many tests, UWOC Systems(underwater wireless optical communication) were able to enhance the technology of video signals in the ocean. UWOC Systems replaced some existing techonolgies that lacked good acoustics and bandwith. The new system allowed for "less power and higher bandwiths" when streaming live videos. These were just a few of the improvements that are ever-changing underwater technology. More specifically, they added items like, "quadrature amplitude modulation". This increased the representation of information to be better understood.
